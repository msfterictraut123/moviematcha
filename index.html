How to use Movie Match

  1. Enter both names and click Continue.
  2. Person 1 adds 5 movies (title + poster image upload).
  3. Hand over the device, then Person 2 adds 5 movies.
  4. Click Start Matching to shuffle all 10 movies.
  5. For each movie, both people vote Yes or No.
  6. If both vote Yes on the same movie, you get a match.
  7. Check the dashboard for the selected movie and round history.
